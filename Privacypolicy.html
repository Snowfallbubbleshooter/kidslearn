Last updated: 13 April 2026

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, address, or phone number directly from users.

However, our app may collect limited non-personal information automatically:

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • App usage data (screens visited, interactions)
  • Approximate location (via IP)

2. Use of Third-Party Services

Google Analytics & Firebase

We use Google Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics to understand app usage.

  • Device identifiers

AdMob (Google Ads)

We use Google AdMob to display ads.

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

All ads are designed to be child-friendly and compliant with Google Play Families Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children

If you believe your child has provided personal data, contact us and we will remove it.
